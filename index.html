  • Spicy Tuna Roll Chicken Salad

    This chicken salad includes so many of the great flavors that I love in Japanese cuisine. It has umami flavors from furikake, miso, and soy, freshness from pop of cucumber, and just a touch of sweet and spice. It’s like eating chicken salad that reminds you of a spicy tuna sushi roll. You’re going to love it.
